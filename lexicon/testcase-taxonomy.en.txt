================================================================================
MODERN CASE TYPES
================================================================================
happy-path           Successful path / intended outcome
validation           Input / form / business validation rejection
boundary             Min/max and neighboring values
authorization        Role allow/deny and privilege checks
state-transition     Legal and illegal status changes
exception            Errors, timeouts, and surfaced failures
concurrency          Multi-user / multi-tab behavior
audit                Audit and logging outcomes
accessibility        Keyboard, screen reader, contrast, focus
localization         Timezone, currency, language, date format
data-integrity       Visible consistency after operations
